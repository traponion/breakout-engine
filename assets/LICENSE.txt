Sample Asset Notice
===================

The character images, sound effects, and other media bundled under
`assets/`, together with the sample mascot dialogue catalog shipped in
source form at `src/i18n/comments.ts`, are sample materials. They ship
with this repository so that the engine has a runnable default
experience, and so that contributors have something concrete to test
rendering and audio against.

Customization is the intended use:

  OK: Edit, replace, or remove these files in your fork.
  OK: Use this engine with your own assets in your own project.
  OK: Use the bundled samples while you are developing or evaluating the
      engine locally.

  Not OK: Redistribute these sample files as standalone asset packs.
  Not OK: Ship a derivative product that uses these sample files unchanged
          as the default mascot, reward visuals, sound set, or voice of
          that product.

In short: the engine is yours to use freely. The bundled samples are a
demo, not a deliverable.

Please bring your own mascot. That's the fun part.
